Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the ZMA ARTCC Training Exam Center ("we", "us") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our exam platform. This platform is operated by ZMA Miami ARTCC, a virtual air traffic control organization within the VATSIM network.

Data We Collect

When you log in and take exams, we collect your VATSIM CID, name, and controller rating as provided by the VATUSA API. We also store your exam answers, scores, and attempt history for training record purposes.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for tracking training progress and exam results within ZMA ARTCC. It is not sold, shared with advertisers, or disclosed to any third parties outside of ZMA ARTCC staff.

Data Storage

Exam results and progress are stored securely in our database. Temporary exam progress may also be stored in your browser's local storage and is automatically cleared when you submit your exam.

Your Rights

You may request the deletion of your exam data at any time by contacting us at ta@zmaartcc.net.

Third-Party Services

This platform uses the VATUSA and VATSIM APIs solely for identity verification. We do not control the data practices of VATSIM or VATUSA. For information on how VATSIM handles your data, please refer to the VATSIM Privacy Policy.
