Welcome to your first step in training with the Miami ARTCC (ZMA)! Please complete this form accurately so you may be placed in the training queue.

This form may only be submitted if you are eligible to join the training queue. Controllers may request only one training progression at a time. Please ensure that you answer all required questions and that each response is truthful and accurate. If it is determined that a controller has joined the queue without meeting eligibility requirements, they will be removed from the training queue until further notice, at the discretion of the Training Administrator.

Before submitting this form, please ensure you have read the Training Policy (T01) in its entirety, as it outlines the training queue, training structure, expectations, and additional important information. If you have questions about what training progression to choose, the T01 answers this!

While in the training queue, consistent self-study is strongly encouraged. Arriving prepared for each training session is required and will help ensure efficient and steady progress through your training.

Please also ensure that you regularly check your VATSIM email. Monthly currency checks will be conducted, and all related communications will be sent via email.

If you have any questions, feel free to ask them in the ZMA Discord under #training-questions (excluding test questions or requests for test answers). You may also reach out via email at [email protected] or contact the Training Administrator directly at [email protected].

On behalf of the entire ZMA Training Staff, we look forward to getting to know you and supporting you throughout your training!
